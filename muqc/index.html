Welcome to MUQC!

We are a Quidditch Club based in Manchester, home to the teams Manchester Manticores and Manchester Minotaurs. While we primarily recruit from the Manchester Universities we welcome players of all backgrounds: graduate, non-student, or otherwise. Every year we compete in QUK tournaments, attend the tournaments arranged by our neighbouring clubs, and host our own tournament Cottonopolis.

What is Quidditch?

Quidditch is a mixed-gender full contact sport which draws on rugby, dodgeball, and tag. If you've never heard of the sport before then USQ have a great summary of the rules on their website. It's great fun, and as a sport and a club we pride ourselves on being inclusive to all regardless of gender, race, or background.

How do I join?

We train in Whitworth Park every

  • Wednesday from 3pm - 4:30pm
  • Saturday from 1pm - 3:30pm
during UoM termtime. You don't have to have played sport before or know the rule, just come down and talk to a member of committee. Wear clothes that you don't mind getting muddy though, it is Manchester after all.
